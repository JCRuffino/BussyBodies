Control as many bus stops as possible across Brighton. The team with the most stops at the end of the game wins.

Tap a stop on the map to open it. Select your team and a value, then press Claim. The value you set is the cost in coins — you must have enough coins to claim it.

Once claimed, a stop cannot revert to unclaimed, and the controlling team cannot increase its value. Another team can take control by claiming the stop at a higher value, paying that value in coins.

Each team starts with 50 coins. Coins are spent when claiming or upgrading stops. You cannot spend more coins than you have.

Coins can be earned by completing challenges.

Challenges appear on stops across the map. Tap the stop and claim the challenge for your team to add it to your held challenges.

Complete the challenge in real life, then resolve it from the Challenges screen to earn coins or steal stops.

If you cannot afford to claim a stop owned by another team, you may declare bankruptcy on that stop.

Doing so means you lose all of your coins, and the team controlling that stop receives coins equal to the stop's current value. The stop's value and ownership do not change.

The bankruptcy button is only active when you cannot afford the stop.

The team that has claimed stops on the most unique bus routes earns a bonus of 5 stops at the end of the game.

The team with the greatest straight-line distance between any two of their owned stops earns a bonus of 5 stops at the end of the game.

The game ends at an agreed time. The team with the highest total stop count — including any bonuses — wins.
